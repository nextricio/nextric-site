# Getting started with affiliate marketing

## Introduction

Affiliate marketing lets you earn by recommending products you believe in. Success comes from trust, not aggressive sales tactics.

## Key Points

1. **Point 1**: Promote products you actually use and trust

2. **Point 2**: Disclose affiliate relationships transparently

3. **Point 3**: Content should help first, sell second

## Practical Steps

- Choose a niche where you have credibility
- Join relevant affiliate programs (Amazon, ShareASale, etc.)
- Create genuinely helpful content around products
- Track which content drives conversions

## Conclusion

Affiliate marketing success comes from authentic recommendations and patience. Focus on helping your audience first.
